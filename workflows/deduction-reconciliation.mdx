Client payouts rarely match deduction requests line for line. The **POD Recon AB** compares both and surfaces exactly what's off — by driver, by category — before anyone has to ask.

## AB · POD Recon

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Watches** | Carrier portals · driver apps · client POD systems |
| **Triggers** | Short delivery · refusal · missing POD · claim window |
| **Memory** | Per-driver and per-customer dispute history |
| **Outputs** | Reconciled sheet · customer email · credit note draft |

## What it finds

| Flag | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| **Over-deduction** | Client took more than requested — driver is owed a refund |
| **Partial deduction** | Client took less — remainder is still outstanding |
| **Pending** | Not yet processed by the client |
| **Missing POD** | Delivery record absent from client portal |

## How to run it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your channel">
    Open the Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram channel where the POD Recon AB is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share both files">
    Attach your original deduction request and the client's payout statement. The AB needs both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the command">
    ```text
    reconcile deductions for this payout
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the reconciled sheet">
    The AB replies with a full breakdown by driver and category — plus a customer email draft and credit note for any over-deductions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What you get

- Drivers flagged for over-deduction, with refund amounts
- Outstanding partial amounts per driver
- Pending deductions not yet applied
- Totals by category
- Customer email draft, ready to approve and send

<Info>
  Teams running this AB have gone from ~85% deduction recovery to 100%.
</Info>
