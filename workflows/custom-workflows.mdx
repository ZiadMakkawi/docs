Bring any procurement or operations loop you'd rather not run by hand. LeedAB solutions engineering works with your team for 1–2 weeks to build a new AB with its own watchers, triggers, memory, and audit trail. It onboards the same way the in-box five do.

**Included with the production tier.**

## What qualifies

Any procurement or operations loop your team runs manually across portals, spreadsheets, or ERPs. Examples from current production deployments:

- Tariff reclassification — watch HTS/HS changes, re-classify affected SKUs, draft customs memo
- Dual-source allocation — monitor two supply sources, rebalance commits against demand signals
- NPI risk — track new-product introductions against component availability and lead times
- Carrier rate benchmarking — pull rates from multiple TMS portals, compare against contracted lanes
- Supplier follow-up — chase open PO acknowledgments across supplier portals and email

If it involves opening surfaces, applying rules, and producing a repeatable output — it qualifies.

## How to get one

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify the loop">
    Pick a procurement task your team runs often. Note which surfaces are involved, what the trigger is, and what the output looks like.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Book a scoping call">
    Contact the LeedAB team. We'll schedule a short session — 30 minutes is usually enough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Walk us through it once">
    Show a LeedAB solutions engineer how your team runs the workflow — which apps, which rules, which templates. One walkthrough is all we need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="We build it">
    Solutions engineering turns the walkthrough into a custom AB with its own watchers, triggers, memory scope, and audit trail. Timeline: 1–2 weeks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Onboards like the in-box five">
    Your team reaches the new AB the same way — via @mention in Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
  </Step>
</Steps>
