Every client sends attendance differently. The AB takes whatever format arrives, normalizes it, and returns one clean, consistent sheet — with problem drivers flagged. No reformatting on your end.

## How to run it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Collect the files">
    Get attendance exports from your clients as they arrive. No reformatting needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach them in your channel">
    Drop the files into your Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram channel. Send multiple files at once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the command">
    ```text
    import attendance for October
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the clean sheet">
    One normalized attendance sheet covering all drivers, with problem drivers flagged automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Output

| Field | What it shows |
| --- | --- |
| **Days present** | Total days worked per driver |
| **Absences** | Total absent days per driver |
| **Late arrivals** | Total late-arrival incidents |
| **Flagged drivers** | 5\+ absences · 3\+ late arrivals · data inconsistencies |

The output sheet stays on your machine. Nothing is uploaded.

<Tip>
  Send files exactly as your clients provide them — Excel, CSV, PDF export, whatever. The AB handles normalization.
</Tip>
