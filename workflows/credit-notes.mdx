The **Deductions AB** watches your client deduction sheets. The moment a monthly batch lands, it sorts each line into a category, produces numbered credit notes, and reconciles the summary by driver and category. Your header, colors, and columns — exactly as they are.

## AB · Deductions

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| **Watches** | Client deduction sheets · monthly invoices · driver records |
| **Triggers** | New deduction batch · dispute window · refund pending |
| **Owns** | Credit note numbering · category math · audit log |
| **Outputs** | Numbered PDFs · summary spreadsheet · refund queue |

## How to run it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to your channel">
    Open the Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram channel where the Deductions AB is active.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Attach the deduction sheet">
    Paste or attach the file. The AB handles any format your client sends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the command">
    ```text
    generate credit notes for October
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get your output">
    The AB replies with one numbered PDF per deduction and a summary sheet. Ready to forward to the client.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Output

| Item | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Numbered PDFs** | One per deduction — lost shipments, traffic fines, vehicle damage, SIMs |
| **Summary sheet** | All deductions reconciled by driver and category |
| **Refund queue** | Flagged items pending client action |

All three use your template exactly. Everything stays on your machine.

<Tip>
  Before the first run, make sure your credit-note template is on the machine running the Brain. The AB reads it once and reuses it every time.
</Tip>
