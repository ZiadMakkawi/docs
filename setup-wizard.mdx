Run `leedab onboard` after installing. A LeedAB engineer pairs with your team to walk through surface enrollment, channel connection, and team access. Days 1–3 of a 14-day deployment.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start">
    ```bash
    leedab onboard
    ```
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose AI models">
    Select which models the Brain uses for reasoning, classification, and screen understanding:

    - Claude (Anthropic)
    - GPT-4o (OpenAI)
    - Gemini (Google)
    - DeepSeek
    - **Local models** — fully on-device; nothing leaves the machine

    For export-controlled deployments, choose local models. Model selection is a deployment-time decision and can be changed at any time without reinstalling.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enroll surfaces">
    Sign into each surface once, like a person. The Brain reuses those sessions continuously.

    - SAP / S/4HANA / Oracle SCM / NetSuite
    - Carrier TMS (Project44, FourKites, direct portals)
    - Supplier portals (direct logins, including 2FA)
    - Inbox (Outlook, Gmail)
    - Compliance feeds (BIS, OFAC, EAR)

    No API contracts. No IT integration tickets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect a channel">
    Pick where your team will reach the ABs:

    - Slack
    - Microsoft Teams
    - WhatsApp
    - Telegram

    The wizard asks for the token or login needed to connect it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add team access">
    Add team members and assign roles:

    | Role | What they can do |
    | --- | --- |
    | **Owner** | Everything — settings, billing, team management |
    | **Admin** | Manage team and ABs, not billing |
    | **Member** | Send tasks, receive results |

    For fine-grained access — buyers, planners, legal, security each seeing only their ABs — see [Access Control](/configuration/access-control).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name your ABs">
    Each AB gets a name your team will see in every reply. You can run multiple ABs with different names, each scoped to different workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Send your first task

Go to your connected channel and send a message to the AB:

```text
@compliance pre-screen PO-44218 before I send it to legal
```

The AB opens the relevant surface, does the work, and replies in the same thread — with citations and an audit link.

<Tip>
  To change your AI models or channels later, see [AI Providers](/configuration/ai-providers) and [Channels](/configuration/channels).
</Tip>
