Your team stays where they are. ABs join the channels your procurement engineers, dispatchers, and operations teams already use. No new app. No new login.

## Which channel to use

| Channel | Works well for |
| --- | --- |
| **Slack** | Procurement, sourcing, and compliance teams — threaded replies, inline approvals |
| **Microsoft Teams** | Enterprise deployments on M365 — channel posts, DMs, adaptive cards |
| **WhatsApp** | Field teams, logistics operators, drivers, back-office |
| **Telegram** | Dispatch and operations — file attachments, inline approvals, bots |

## How to connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open channel settings">
    ```bash
    leedab setup
    ```

    Or go to **Settings → Channels** if the Brain is already running.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick your channel">
    Select Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize">
    - **Slack** — click the OAuth link and approve the Brain in your workspace.
    - **Microsoft Teams** — install the LeedAB app from the Teams App Store and authorize.
    - **WhatsApp** — scan the QR code with your phone.
    - **Telegram** — create a bot via [@BotFather](https://t.me/BotFather), copy the token, paste it in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it">
    Send a message to the AB. You should get a reply in a few seconds. If not, re-run setup and check the channel step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  No new logins for anyone. ABs join the surfaces your team already has on their phone or desktop.
</Tip>

## Who can send tasks

Only people you've added in [Access Control](/configuration/access-control) will get a response. ABs ignore messages from unrecognized handles.

## Multiple channels

Connect all four at once — for example, procurement on Slack, compliance on Teams, dispatchers on Telegram, drivers on WhatsApp. All channels reach the same ABs and workflows.

To add another channel: **Settings → Channels → Add Channel**.
