Every team member gets a role. Approvals are scoped, time-bounded, and revocable from your SSO. Buyers, planners, legal, and security each see only the surfaces and ABs they're cleared for.

## Roles

| Role | Send tasks | View results | Manage ABs | Manage team | Billing |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Owner** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| **Admin** | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | — |
| **Member** | ✓ | Own activity | — | — | — |

One Owner per installation. Admins can manage everything except billing. Members can send tasks and see their own results.

## SSO

SAML, SCIM, and MFA are supported. Connect your IdP from **Settings → Security → SSO**.

## Add a team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open team settings">
    ```bash
    leedab setup
    ```

    Or go to **Settings → Team** if the Brain is already running.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their details">
    Name and messaging handle (Slack ID, Teams UPN, Telegram username, or WhatsApp number).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a role">
    Owner, Admin, or Member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    They can send tasks immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Change a role

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the person.
3. Pick a new role and save.

Takes effect on their next message.

## AB-level permissions

You can restrict which ABs a Member can reach. For example, legal sees Compliance only; dispatch sees POD Recon and Control Tower only.

Configure from **Settings → Team → \[Member\] → AB Access**.

## Remove a member

1. Go to **Settings → Team**.
2. Find the person and click **Remove**.

Their messages will no longer get a response.

<Note>
  If someone changes their Slack handle, Teams UPN, phone number, or Telegram account, update it in **Settings → Team** or the Brain won't recognize them.
</Note>
