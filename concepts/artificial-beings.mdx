Artificial Beings (ABs) are agentic workflow units that run inside the [Brain](/concepts/computer-use). Each one owns a specific procurement loop — allocation, export compliance, POD reconciliation, deductions, supplier risk — with persistent memory, continuous monitoring, and a full audit trail.

They are not chatbots. They do not answer questions. They do the work.

## Five ABs ship out of the box

| AB | Role | Watches | Outputs |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Control Tower** | Reconciles allocation across supplier commits, carrier capacity, and customer commitments | Supplier portals · carrier TMS · inbound ETAs · PCNs | Live state · weekly digest · escalation memo |
| **POD Recon** | Flags shorted, refused, and undelivered loads same-day | Carrier portals · driver apps · client POD systems | Reconciled sheet · customer email · credit note draft |
| **Deductions** | Sorts monthly deduction batches, produces numbered credit notes, reconciles by driver | Client deduction sheets · monthly invoices · driver records | Numbered PDFs · summary spreadsheet · refund queue |
| **Risk Monitor** | Walks your supplier map to tier 2 and tier 3, watches for geopolitical and disaster exposure | Tier-2/3 supplier moves · fab geographies · NHC · USGS | Exposure report · second-source recommendation |
| **Compliance** | Pre-flights every order against BIS Entity List, OFAC SDN, EAR ECCN, FDP rule, and customs filings | BIS · OFAC · EAR · FDP · HTS/HS · ACE | Pass / flag / escalate · memo with citations |

Custom ABs are scoped and shipped in **1–2 weeks**.

## How an AB handles a task

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trigger">
    A team member sends a task in Slack, Teams, WhatsApp, or Telegram — or the AB triggers itself from a watched surface.
  </Step>
  <Step title="The AB opens the right surface">
    It logs into the relevant portal, TMS, ERP, or inbox — the same way your buyer would, including 2FA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Work">
    Pulls records, reconciles data, fills forms, drafts memos — using the same tools your team uses. No APIs.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Output">
    Returns the result in the same thread: a summary, attached files, and any flags that need a decision.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approval loop">
    For anything requiring sign-off, the AB waits inline. Reply "approve" and it continues. Every decision is logged.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  ABs run 24/7 with persistent memory. They remember the last state of every supplier commit, every driver's dispute history, and every open deduction — across sessions.
</Note>

## AB vs. chatbot

|  | Chatbot | Artificial Being |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Answers questions | Yes | When useful |
| Opens portals and apps | No | Yes |
| Logs into systems with 2FA | No | Yes |
| Holds persistent memory | No | Yes |
| Produces files and reports | No | Yes |
| Maintains an audit trail | No | Yes |
| Triggers from watched surfaces | No | Yes |
