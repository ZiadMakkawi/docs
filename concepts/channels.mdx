ABs meet your team in the channels they already live in. No new app. No new login. Your procurement engineers stay in Slack; your dispatchers stay in WhatsApp. The AB is one @mention away.

## Supported channels

| Channel | Best for |
| --- | --- |
| **Slack** | Procurement and sourcing teams — threaded replies, inline approvals, file attachments |
| **Microsoft Teams** | Enterprise deployments already on M365 |
| **WhatsApp** | Field teams, logistics operators, drivers |
| **Telegram** | Dispatch and back-office, POD confirmation, deduction workflows |

## What a channel interaction looks like

```text
Diana M. · #procurement-screen · 9:14
@compliance can you pre-screen PO-44218 before I send it to legal?
It's the Astera order with the new EU distributor.

Export-Compliance AB · 9:14
Pulled the line items. Screened against BIS, EAR, and FDP. One flag.
The distributor's freight-forwarder routes through an entity affiliated
with a 1769 BIS add (Jan 2026).

  Part      LDB-AL-7300-A · 240 units
  ECCN      3A090.a · controlled
  End-use   AI training cluster · cleared
  FDP rule  Not triggered
  Entity    Forwarder · affiliated · Jan 2026 add

  [Block & escalate]  [Re-route]  [Open memo]

Diana M. · 9:15
Re-route through DHL CN. Update legal with the memo.
```

Every AB reply includes citations and an audit link. Every approval is logged.

## How ABs appear in channels

ABs appear as named users in your workspace or group — for example, **Export-Compliance AB** or a custom name you set during [onboarding](/setup-wizard). Your team @mentions them the same way they'd @mention a colleague.

<Note>
  No one needs a new app or a new login. ABs join the surfaces your team already has on their phone or computer.
</Note>

See [Channel configuration](/configuration/channels) for step-by-step connection instructions.
